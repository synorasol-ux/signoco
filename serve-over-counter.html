Professional Service Counter Design

Our serve over counters are specifically engineered for operations where staff serve customers directly from refrigerated display areas.

These counters feature a unique dual-access design that allows staff easy access to products from their side while customers can clearly view merchandise. The low-profile design facilitates interaction and service while maintaining optimal refrigeration conditions.

Dual-access design for staff and customer convenience
Stainless steel work surface for hygiene and durability
Adjustable shelving and dividers for product organization
Integrated cutting boards and serving utensils storage
Energy-efficient LED lighting with anti-glare design
