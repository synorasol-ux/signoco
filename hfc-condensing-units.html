Installation Guide

Professional installation ensures optimal performance and longevity

Guía de Instalación

La instalación profesional garantiza un rendimiento óptimo y longevidad

1

Site Preparation

Ensure proper foundation, ventilation, and electrical connections are available. Clear access for installation and future maintenance.

2

Unit Placement

Position unit according to manufacturer specifications with adequate clearance for airflow and maintenance access.

3

Piping & Connections

Connect refrigerant lines, electrical wiring, and water lines (if applicable) following industry standards and local codes.

4

Commissioning

System testing, refrigerant charging, control setup, and performance verification by certified technicians.
